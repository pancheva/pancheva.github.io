About

I received my PhD in Linguistics from the University of Pennsylvania in 2000. From 1998 to 2000, I worked as a research associate in the Brain and Language Lab in the Department of Neuroscience at Georgetown University. I have been at USC since 2000. My research areas are syntax, semantics, and their interface.
